All About Kangaroos!

Kangaroos are the best!

Kangaroos are exceptional animals that are unique in many ways. From their robust ears to their strange pouch, these animals are very interesting.

Kangaroos, known as "roos" in their native Australia, are extremely powerful creatures. They have long, strong back feet that can help them leap and deliever a powerful kick.


34.4 million kangaroos, also called "wallaroos", are known to live Down Under. They are well known animals in Australia, and are even featured on the Coat of Arms and currency.
